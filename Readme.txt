Integrate InXpress Shipping in zencart
----------------------------------------

Zen Cart Settings:
1. 	Take backup of your server file  "adminDir\includes\modules\product\collect_info.php"
	Copy All files in your zencart directory and copy "admin" directory files to your adminDirectory
3. Sign in to your ZenCart admin
4. Click Modules
5. Click Shipping
6. Click Install on Inxpress

After successful installation you will receive an email from InXpress with an attached account application form for your DHL account. Complete the application and return it to InXpress. You will need a credit card to secure the DHL account. Within 3 business days after submitting the application, you will receive your DHL Account and the Extension will automatically activate.
